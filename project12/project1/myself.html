ALL ABOUT ME

Emmanuel Osei Bugyei is my name; I am a Fante from Ghana in West Africa. I am a Building Technologist and Computer Hardware Technician by Profession. I worked at Ghana Education Service as a core mathematics teacher and an Information Communication Technology instructor at the senior and junior high school levels respectively and also as a system administrator at Alliance Francaise de Takoradi before moving to Finland to further my education in Business Information Technology.
